## üîç Key New Jersey Legal Concepts

### NJ Real Estate Commission Authority
The NJREC has power to:
- Issue, suspend, and revoke real estate licenses
- Establish and enforce rules (N.J.A.C. 11:5)
- Investigate complaints against licensees
- Impose fines and disciplinary actions

### NJ Agency Disclosure Requirements
- Must be provided **at first substantive contact**
- Must be in **writing**
- Must use **Consumer Information Statement** form
- Must be **signed and acknowledged** by all parties

### NJ Property Condition Disclosure
- Required for residential properties (1-4 units)
- Must be provided **before contract signing**
- Covers known material defects
- Does not replace inspections

### NJ Environmental Regulations
- **CAFRA**: Coastal Area Facility Review Act
- **Freshwater Wetlands Protection Act**
- **Flood Hazard Area Control Act**
- **Pinelands Protection Act**

## üìä Practice Question Examples

### Example 1: NJ License Law
**Question**: Under N.J.A.C. 11:5, how many hours of continuing education are required for a salesperson to renew their license?

**Answer Choices**:
A. 12 hours every year
B. 24 hours every 2 years
C. 36 hours every 3 years
D. No continuing education required

**Correct Answer**: A
**Explanation**: New Jersey requires 12 hours of continuing education annually for salesperson license renewal, with specific topics mandated by the NJ Real Estate Commission.

### Example 2: NJ Agency Disclosure
**Question**: When must a New Jersey real estate licensee provide the Consumer Information Statement to a prospective buyer?

**Answer Choices**:
A. At the closing
B. Before showing any properties
C. At first substantive contact
D. When the buyer makes an offer

**Correct Answer**: C
**Explanation**: The Consumer Information Statement must be provided at the first substantive contact with a prospective buyer or seller, which is defined as the first in-person meeting to discuss a specific property.

### Example 3: NJ Environmental Law
**Question**: Which of the following activities in the New Jersey Pinelands requires a development permit?

**Answer Choices**:
A. Minor interior home renovation
B. Construction of a single-family home
C. Routine agricultural activities
D. All of the above

**Correct Answer**: B
**Explanation**: Construction of a single-family home in the Pinelands Area requires a development permit under the Pinelands Protection Act, as it may impact the sensitive ecosystem.
