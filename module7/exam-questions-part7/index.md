# Challenging Real Estate Questions: Ownership, Easements & Leases

## **Question Types Based on Your Areas of Difficulty**

### **Type 1: MLS/Timeline Questions**

**1. New Jersey listing broker obtains a signed listing agreement on Friday at 3 PM with an effective date of Monday at 9 AM. What's the deadline for MLS notification?**
- A) Monday at 3 PM
- B) Tuesday at 9 AM
- C) Wednesday at 9 AM
- D) Wednesday at 3 PM

**Answer: C**  
*48 hours from effective date, not signing date.*

### **Type 2: Listing Agreement Types**

**2. A seller wants to reserve the right to sell to her cousin without paying commission. Which listing?**
- A) Exclusive right to sell
- B) Exclusive agency
- C) Open listing
- D) Net listing

**Answer: B**  
*Exclusive agency: broker gets paid unless seller finds buyer.*

**3. A broker can collect commission only if she finds the buyer. Which listing?**
- A) Exclusive right to sell
- B) Exclusive agency
- C) Open listing
- D) Net listing

**Answer: C**  
*Open listing: no exclusivity, only pays if that broker sells.*

### **Type 4: Mortgage Qualification**

**6. Couple has $9,000 monthly income, $800 car payment, $300 student loan. Housing ratio 28%, total DTI 36%. Max mortgage payment?**

- B) $2,320 using total DTI
- C) $2,120 using total DTI
- D) Both A and C, lender uses lower

**Answer: D**  
*$9,000 × 0.28 = $2,520; $9,000 × 0.36 = $3,240 - $1,100 = $2,140; lower is $2,140.*

## **Answer Analysis & Study Tips**

### **Common Themes in Your Errors:**

1. **Timing Questions:** Focus on "effective date" vs "signing date"
2. **Agency Relationships:** Remember transactional vs client-level services
3. **Specific NJ Rules:** Truth in Renting, Guaranty Fund, CIS timing
4. **Calculations:** Practice tax, DTI, commission math
5. **Legal Distinctions:** Easement types, deed requirements, notice types

### **Study Strategy:**

1. **Create Flashcards** for:
   - NJ-specific deadlines
   - Agency relationship rules
   - Contract formation elements
   - Deed requirements

2. **Practice Calculations** daily:
   - Property taxes
   - Mortgage qualification
   - Commission splits
   - Prorations

3. **Focus on Distinctions:**
   - Actual vs constructive vs inquiry notice
   - Express vs implied vs agency by estoppel
   - Standard vs extended title coverage
   - Compensatory vs liquidated damages

4. **Use Mnemonics:**
   - DEED: Description, Execution, Effective date, Delivery
   - ARM: Adjustment Rate Mortgage (index + margin)
   - PMI: Private Mortgage Insurance (for <20% down)

### **Test-Taking Tips:**

1. **Read Carefully:** Watch for "effective date" vs "signed date"
2. **Eliminate Absolutes:** "Always," "never," "all," "none" are often wrong
3. **NJ-Specific:** When in doubt, choose NJ-specific answer
4. **Process of Elimination:** Eliminate 2 clearly wrong answers first
5. **Time Management:** 110 questions in 150 minutes = ~1.4 min/question
