# New Jersey Real Estate Exam Prep Practice Questions

Below are practice questions similar to those you might see on the New Jersey real estate licensing exam. Answers are provided below each question for immediate review.

---

### Question 1  
"Reciprocal licensing" _______.  

A. Doesn't exist in New Jersey  
B. Is permitted in New Jersey with restrictions  
C. Requires other states to offer reciprocal licensing, as well  
D. Took the place of license recognition in New Jersey  

**Correct Answer**: D. Took the place of license recognition in New Jersey  

---

### Question 2  
What's one benefit of a rural development loan?  

A. It doesn't have to be repaid.  
B. It includes marketing assistance for farmers.  
C. Its optional longer term means a lower monthly payment.  
D. Its optional shorter term means a quicker payoff.  

**Correct Answer**: C. Its optional longer term means a lower monthly payment.  

---

### Question 3  
What well-known economic principle says that a property’s value is determined by what it would cost to purchase a similar property?  

A. Conforming value  
B. Regression  
C. Similar cost principle  
D. Substitution  

**Correct Answer**: D. Substitution  

---

### Question 4  
Which of these transfers would be exempt from New Jersey's realty transfer fee?  

A. A commercial property sale  
B. A deed in which consideration is less than $100  
C. A transfer from a lender to a borrower who paid off a loan  
D. The sale of farm ground with an accompanying residence  

**Correct Answer**: C. A transfer from a lender to a borrower who paid off a loan  

---

### Question 5  
What economic principle is related to a property’s most profitable use that is both legal and economically feasible?  

A. Anticipation  
B. Contribution  
C. Highest and best use  
D. Progression  

**Correct Answer**: C. Highest and best use  

---

### Question 6  
What does PITI stand for?  

A. Price, information, trust, interest  
B. Price, interest, taxes, insurance  
C. Principal, interest, taxes, insurance  
D. Public Insurance Trust Information  

**Correct Answer**: C. Principal, interest, taxes, insurance  

---

### Question 7  
Sierra is a New Jersey real estate licensee representing Alex in the sale of his condo. They receive an offer from another licensee, Jason, on behalf of his buyer client, Will. To whom does Sierra owe some form of agency-related disclosure?  

A. Sierra doesn’t owe agency-related disclosures to any transaction party, but is required to provide them if asked.  
B. Sierra only owes agency-related disclosures to her client, Alex, and the customer in the transaction, Will.  
C. Sierra only owes agency-related disclosures to the client she represents, Alex.  
D. Sierra owes agency-related disclosures to her client, Alex, any other consumers in the transaction, including Will, and her fellow licensee, Jason.  

**Correct Answer**: D. Sierra owes agency-related disclosures to her client, Alex, any other consumers in the transaction, including Will, and her fellow licensee, Jason.  

---

### Question 8  
New Jersey licensee Clara was found guilty of misrepresentation by the New Jersey Real Estate Commission. Since it is her first offense, what is the maximum fine that she can expect to be levied?  

A. $1,000  
B. $10,000  
C. $2,000  
D. $5,000  

**Correct Answer**: D. $5,000
