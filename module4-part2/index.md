### Contract Math & Damage Calculations (Questions 46–55)

**Question 46**
A buyer agrees to purchase a home for $750,000 with a 5% commission. The listing agreement splits commission 50/50 between listing and selling brokers. The selling salesperson receives a 70% split from their broker. How much does the selling salesperson earn?
**Answer Choices:**
A. $13,125
B. $18,750
C. $26,250
D. $37,500
**Correct Answer:** A. $13,125
*(Calculation: Total commission = $750,000 × 0.05 = $37,500. Selling broker’s share = $37,500 × 0.50 = $18,750. Salesperson’s share = $18,750 × 0.70 = $13,125.)*
